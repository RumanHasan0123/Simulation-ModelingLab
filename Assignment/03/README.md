Assigment Task :  
A customer service center studies how long customers wait before being served.  
They suspect:  
 On regular days, customers arrive randomly and wait times are uniformly distributed  
between 2 and 12 minutes.  
 On busy days, wait times follow a normal distribution with an average (mean) of 8  
minutes and a standard deviation of 2 minutes.  
Your task is to simulate and analyze both situations using Python.  

Problem Statement  
Write a Python program to simulate and compare customer wait times for regular and busy days.  

Tasks  
🔹 Part 1 — Data Generation  
1. Generate 3,000 wait times using a uniform distribution between 2 and 12 minutes.  
2. Generate 3,000 wait times using a normal distribution with:  
o Mean = 8  
o Std Dev = 2  
3. Ensure no negative wait times in the normal distribution (replace negatives with 0).  

🔹 Part 2 — Visualization  
1. Plot histograms for both distributions.  
2. Show them:  
o In the same figure (two subplots), OR  
o Overlaid with transparency.  

🔹 Part 3 — Statistical Analysis  
For both datasets calculate:  
 Mean  
 Median  
 Standard deviation  
 Maximum wait time
